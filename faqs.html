Designation One is a platform connecting charities and foundations embracing inclusivity with talent from underrepresented groups wanting to serve on the board of directors/trustees, or governors.

Charities, non-profi ts, and foundations register on the platform and can posttheir board vacancies with a description of the expertise sought in board members. Individuals can register and post their profi les, expertise, and resumes and search the dashboard for open board vacancies

We offer a trove of information and self-directed learning to prepare individuals for a board position in the resourced section. In the future, we are planning online and in-person pieces of training, networking opportunities, and coaching for organizations. We also work with hand-selected executive coaches that can provide additional training if so desired.

As an individual, you can sign up for regular notifi cations based on your expertise or interest and will receive them into your inbox. You can also research our dashboard for vacancies.
