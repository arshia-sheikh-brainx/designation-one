ABOUT US

After working closely with the philanthropic and non-profit sectors, we observed, that organisations continue to be set up from homogenous groups, particularly at the board level. We believe there is an opportunity to support organisations in having a more diverse board and inclusive practices. To achieve our desire for boardrooms to reflect the full diversity of our society, we have created a platform that assists in the recruitment and advancement of underrepresented talent on boards. In addition, the platform serves as a portal to share inclusive practices and provides guidance on how to achieve these.

Our mission is driven by the belief of a future where leadership levels reflect our society; to allow talent to advance and provide opportunities to make positive contributions and prepare them for service on for-profit boards. We hope you will join us in this effort!
