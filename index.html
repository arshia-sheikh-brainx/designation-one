ABOUT US

After working closely with the philanthropic and non-profit sectors, we observed, that organisations continue to be set up from homogenous groups, particularly at the board level. We believe there is an opportunity to support organisations in having a more diverse board and inclusive practices. To achieve our desire for boardrooms to reflect the full diversity of our society, we have created a platform that assists in the recruitment and advancement of underrepresented talent on boards. In addition, the platform serves as a portal to share inclusive practices and provides guidance on how to achieve these. Our mission is driven by the belief of a future where leadership levels reflect our society; to allow talent to advance and provide opportunities to make positive contributions and prepare them for service on for-profit boards. We
hope you will join us in this effort!

Corporate Social Responsibility

Designation One is proud to partner with Southall Black Sisters, a London-based charity providing information, advice, advocacy, practical help, counselling, and support to women and children experiencing domestic and sexual violence, including sex trafficking, dowry abuse, and forced marriage. Southall Black Sisters also supports friends and relatives attempting to assist a woman or child experiencing violence and abuse. Southall Black Sisters focuses on Black, Asian, and Minority Ethnics. A percentage of every membership will go directly to Southall Black Sisters to support their work with the most vulnerable of our societies.
